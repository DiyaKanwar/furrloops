# FurrLoops Website

## Overview
FurrLoops is a luxurious yet approachable e-commerce website for handmade crochet pet accessories. The brand creates handcrafted crochet products for cats, dogs, birds, and small animals.

**Tagline:** Cozy looks, Tiny loops, Endless love since forever.

**Brand Mission:** Made in India, Looped for the World.

**Location:** Based in Gurugram, Haryana, India

**Team:**
- Founder & Creative Director: Deepanjalee (born 2004), Applied Artist
- Lead Artisan: Ms. Rajkumari Tanwer (40+ years of crochet mastery)

## Website Structure

### Pages
1. **Home (index.html)** - Hero section, featured categories, brand story
2. **Shop (shop.html)** - Product catalog with category filters
3. **Gallery (gallery.html)** - Visual showcase of products and crafting process
4. **About (about.html)** - Brand story, values, and mission
5. **Blog (blog.html)** - Articles about crochet, pet styling, sustainability
6. **Contact (contact.html)** - Contact form, social links, newsletter signup

### Key Features
- Fully responsive design (mobile and desktop)
- Smooth transitions and hover effects
- Category-based product filtering
- Interactive navigation with mobile menu
- **Half-Price Sale Campaign** with countdown timer and special offers
- Sale badges on all products with old/new pricing
- Bundle offers (Buy 2, Get 1 Free)
- Social media integration with Instagram exclusive offer
- Newsletter subscription
